Szechuan Chicken Recipe

Desciption

Szechaun chicken is known as being the best type of chicken one can make. It is one of Chai Woks best dishes and has been famously featured on shows like Rick and Morty.

This simple szechuan chicken recipe will take about 30 mins to complete and will serve 4 poeple.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 3 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of vegatable oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 5 tablespoons of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  • 3 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. Place the chicken and cornstarch into a bag or bowl, and toss to coat. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken pieces and garlic, stirring constantly until lightly browned. Stir in the soy sauce, vinegar, sugar and water. Cover, and cook until the chicken pieces are no longer pink inside, 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in the green onion, and cayenne pepper, cook uncovered for about 2 more minutes. Serve over white rice.
